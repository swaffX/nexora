# Privacy Policy for Nexora

**Last Updated:** January 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Nexora ("we", "our", or "us") connects Discord users with their Riot Games accounts to provide statistics, matchmaking features, and verifying account ownership via Riot Sign On (RSO). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Information We Collect
When you use Nexora, specifically the Valorant integration features, we may collect the following data:

*   **Discord Information:** User ID, Username, and Avatar.
*   **Riot Games Information:**
    *   Riot ID (Game Name and Tag Line)
    *   PUUID (Player Universally Unique Identifier)
    *   Match History and Statistics (Rank, MMR, recent matches)
    *   Region information

**We DO NOT collect or store:**
*   Your Riot Games password or login credentials.
*   Payment information.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
1.  **Verification:** To verify that the Discord user owns the linked Riot Games account.
2.  **Statistics:** To display your Valorant rank, match history, and performance stats within the Discord server.
3.  **Matchmaking:** To balance teams in our internal 5v5 scrim system based on player ranks.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
*   Your Riot PUUID and Discord ID linkage is stored in our secure database (MongoDB).
*   We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
*   We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

## 5. Third-Party Services
*   **Riot Games API:** We use Riot Games API to fetch game data. By using Nexora, you also acknowledge and agree to Riot Games' [Privacy Policy](https://www.riotgames.com/en/privacy-notice).

## 6. User Rights
You have the right to:
*   Unlink your account at any time using the `/unlink` command.
*   Request deletion of your data from our database contacting the server administrators.

## 7. Contact
For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Nexora development team via our Discord server.
